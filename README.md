# 🌍 Carbon Footprint Tracker

A comprehensive, single-file web application that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

## 🎯 Features

### 📊 Tracking
- **25+ Activity Types** across 5 categories:
  - 🚗 Transportation (Car, Bus, Train, Flight)
  - ⚡ Energy (Electricity, Gas, Heating/AC)
  - 🥗 Food (Meat, Dairy, Vegetables, Coffee)
  - 🛍️ Lifestyle (Clothes, Shopping, Laundry, Water, Beauty, Waste)
  - 📱 Screen Time (Phone, Laptop, Desktop, TV, Gaming, Tablet)

### 💡 Insights
- **Real-time carbon footprint calculation**
- **Personalized reduction tips** based on your biggest contributors
- **Trees to offset** calculator
- **Goal tracking** (500 kg CO₂/year target)

### 📚 Education
- What is a carbon footprint?
- Why it matters (dangers of climate change)
- 100-year prediction (with vs. without action)
- Pros & Cons of reducing emissions

### 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Climate Heroes
- Profiles of 3 featured climate activists
- 8+ additional climate champions

## 🏆 Hackathon Submission

**Challenge:** Design a solution that helps individuals understand, track, and reduce their carbon footprint through simple actions and personalized insights.

**Unique Features:**
- Screen time tracking (electricity consumption from devices)
- Lifestyle categories (clothing, shopping, beauty products)
- Single HTML file - zero dependencies

## 📱 How to Use

1. Open `index.html` in any modern browser
2. Log your daily activities
3. Track your progress
4. Get personalized reduction tips
5. Learn about climate change and heroes

## 🎯 Goal

The app sets a target of **500 kg CO₂/year** - a sustainable lifestyle goal.

## 🌳 Impact Visualization

Trees needed to offset = Total emissions ÷ 20 (1 tree absorbs ~20 kg CO₂/year)
