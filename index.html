📚 Understanding Carbon Footprint

🌱 What is a Carbon Footprint?

A carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases generated by our actions - measured in kg CO₂ equivalent.

📊 Average footprints:
🌍 Global: 4,000 kg CO₂/year
🇮🇳 India: 1,900 kg CO₂/year
🇺🇸 USA: 16,000 kg CO₂/year
🎯 Sustainable: 500 kg CO₂/year

🛍️ Hidden Contributors You Might Miss

👕 Fast Fashion

1 new t-shirt = ~10 kg CO₂

📦 Online Shopping

1 delivery = ~1-2 kg CO₂

🚿 Hot Water

10 min shower = ~0.5 kg CO₂

☕ Daily Coffee

1 cup = ~0.1 kg CO₂

🧴 Beauty Products

1 item = ~5-10 kg CO₂

🧺 Laundry

1 load = ~0.5-2 kg CO₂

⚠️ Why Should We Care?

🔥 The Dangers

  • 🌡️ Global temperature rise (already +1.1°C)
  • 🌊 Sea level rise - coastal cities flooding
  • 🌀 Extreme weather - hurricanes, droughts
  • 🐾 1 million species at risk of extinction
  • 🌾 Food scarcity and crop failures
  • 🫁 7 million deaths/year from air pollution

🔮 100 Years From Now

❌ If We DON'T Act

  • 🌡️ +4°C warming
  • 🌊 1m sea level rise
  • 🏝️ Islands disappear
  • 🌾 50% crop loss
  • 🏙️ 200M refugees

✅ If We ACT NOW

  • 🌡️ +1.5°C (manageable)
  • 🌳 Net-zero by 2050
  • ☀️ 100% renewable
  • 🌱 Green jobs boom
  • 🌍 Resilient future

⚖️ Pros & Cons

✅ Pros of Reducing

  • Cleaner air, healthier people
  • Save money on energy
  • New green jobs
  • Protect nature
  • Better quality of life

❌ Challenges

  • Initial costs (EV, solar)
  • Lifestyle changes
  • Industry transition
  • Political resistance
